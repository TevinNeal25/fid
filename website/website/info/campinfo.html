Camp Information

Cost is $25 per child, cash or check only accepted at Wes Crile or in the Deltona Parks & Recreation Office at City Hall, 2345 Providence Boulevard. Participants are asked to bring a towel and an extra shirt. For more information, email Sebastian Singletary, ssingletary@deltonafl.gov or call (386) 878-8940.

  • Basketball skills for the week:
  • ball handling
  • defense
  • team work
  • agility
  • shooting

    This camp is for kids that love basketball and who are just beginning to learn the game and also those who are advanced that play competitively. Our camp is a great place to be and you won't find a better one for this price.
