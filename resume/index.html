Tevin Neal

Fine Art

Tevin Neal is a full-time student at Seminole State college of Florida studying fine art and merging media.
His courses consist of learning the fundamentals of 2-D, 3-D, and interactive design to develop his skills to
create works of art for future companies and clients. He has a clear focus and is a fast learner when introduced
to new software and material such as photoshop, sculpting, matte board cutting, etc. Tevin submitted and was accept
to the 2019 Seminole State College student art show with his very first entry in the program. A part from school, he
practices designing art for music producers, parks & recreation centers, and editing film for other students in the
central Florida area. He displays his continued work online through varios social media accounts to learn from and
interact with other media platfroms in the community to advance his knowledge and apply it for his career.
